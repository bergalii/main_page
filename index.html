About Me

I currently reside in Hamburg, Germany, pursuing a Master’s degree in Mechatronics at the Hamburg University of Technology (TUHH). My journey began with a background in mechanical engineering, but towards the end of my BSc, I discovered a strong passion for coding and software development. Over the past three years, I have immersed myself in programming, blending my skills in both mechanical and software engineering.

After I have worked on the software side of an underwater vehicle project, where I learned about the Robot Operating System (ROS), and writing algorithms for robot manipulation in Python, my interest for robotics grew, that's why I've decided to apply for the program I'm currently in. Meanwhile studying here, I have developed a deep interest in machine learning applications, particularly in computer vision, which I aim to focus on in my future career. Currently, I am concentrating on keeping myself up-to-date with ML technologies, meanwhile doing hands-on projects to learn how ML project steps (data collection, training, deployment) work in production.

In addition to my technical pursuits, I am passionate about watching basketball, traveling, learning about history of the places I go and knowing about scientific facts.
